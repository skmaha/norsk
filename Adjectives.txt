# Adjectives
Ei pen jente - den pene jenta | A pretty girl - the pretty girl
pene jenter - de pene jentene | pretty girls - the pretty girls

en stor bil - den store bilen | a big car - the big car
stor biler - de store bilene | big cars - the big cars

et fin brev - det fine brevet | a nice letter - the nice letter
fine breve - de fine brevene | nice letters - the nice letters
